Inside the Innovative World
of Decocoa Chocolate Crafters

At Decocoa Chocolate Crafters, chocolate is more than a product—it’s an experience.

In the latest installment of our For The Love of Making Chocolate series, we explore how this innovative Rochester-based chocolatier is blending artistry, engineering, manufacturing, and storytelling to create unforgettable chocolate experiences.

From highly detailed custom molded chocolates and immersive "food illusion" creations to plans for a future chocolate museum and experience center, Decocoa is challenging conventional ideas about what a chocolate company can be.

Along the way, they share insights into their creative process, commitment to sustainability, passion for innovation, and how Micelli molds help transform ambitious concepts into beautifully crafted products.
